# Skill: Copywriting (Technical Encyclopedia)

[Back to README](../../README.md)

Comprehensive technical protocols for the design and orchestration of high-conversion text assets utilizing behavioral psychology and linguistics in the 2025 ecosystem. This document defines the standards for conversion frameworks (AIDA, PAS), neuro-linguistic programming (NLP) techniques, and quantitative friction reduction math.

---

## 1. Conversion Logic Frameworks
Standardizing the structural "Flow" of persuasive text.

### 1.1 AIDA Framework (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
*   **Attention:** The critical 3-second window to stop the user's scroll.
*   **Interest:** Deep-diving into the "Problem Space."
*   **Desire:** Proposing the "Solution Space" as the unique mechanism.
*   **Action:** Directing the user to a singular, frictionless next step.

### 1.2 PAS Framework (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
Utilizing "Agitation" to maximize the perceived value of the solution by deep-diving into the negative externalities of the problem.

## 2. Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) in Copy
Utilizing specific linguistic patterns to influence perception and behavior.

### 2.1 The "Power Word" Standard
*   **Logic:** Utilizing words that trigger primitive biological responses (e.g., "Free", "New", "Limited", "Discover").
*   **Lexical Density:** Targeting 3-5% power-word density in sales copy to maintain energy without appearing promotional.

### 2.2 Semantic Framing Protocols
Positioning features as "Benefits" using the **"So What?" Test**.
*   *Feature:* "8-core CPU."
*   *Benefit:* "Process your 4K video exports 3x faster, so you can finish work by 5 PM."

---

## 3. Friction Reduction Math
The engineering of "Smoothness" in the user journey.

### 3.1 Cognitive Friction Analysis
*   **Protocol:** Measuring the number of "Decisions" a user must make before completing a CTA.
*   **Rule of One:** A single CTA, a single offer, and a single primary benefit per asset to minimize decision fatigue.

### 3.2 Visual Copy Formatting
*   **Bucket Brigades:** Utilizing transition phrases (e.g., "But that's not all...", "Here's the problem:") to "pull" the reader's eye down the page.
*   **Scanability:** 80% of users scan before reading. Mandatory use of bolding, bullet points, and short (1-2 sentence) paragraphs.

## 6. Glossary of Copywriting Terms
*   **CTA (Call to Action):** The primary instruction to the user.
*   **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** The percentage of users who clicked the asset.
*   **Scarcity:** The psychological state of an offer being limited in time or quantity.
*   **Social Proof:** Evidence that others have successfully used the product (e.g., testimonials).

---

## 7. Mathematical Foundations: The Conversion Value
*   **Formula:** $CV = (R - C) \cdot CR$, where $R$ is Revenue per customer, $C$ is Cost per acquisition, and $CR$ is Conversion Rate.
*   **Application:** In 2025, Moltbot uses this formula to prioritize which copy variations to generate for high-budget campaigns.

## 8. Troubleshooting & Performance Verification
*   **High Bounce Rate:** Users leave immediately. *Fix: Align the headline more closely with the source ad/link.*
*   **Form Abandonment:** Users start but don't finish. *Fix: Remove 50% of the input fields.*
