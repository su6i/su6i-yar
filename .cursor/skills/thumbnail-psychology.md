# Skill: Thumbnail & Visual Psychology (Technical Encyclopedia)

[Back to README](../../README.md)

Comprehensive technical protocols for the design of high-conversion thumbnails and visual assets utilizing cognitive psychology and neural data analysis in the 2025 ecosystem. This document defines the standards for eye-tracking optimization, CIELAB color grading, and Bayesian A/B testing models.

---

## 1. Eye-Tracking & Foveal Attention Math
Optimizing the layout based on how the human brain scans visual information.

### 1.1 Heatmap Optimization Protocols
*   **The "F" Pattern:** Aligning the "Hook" (Text/Face) along the top-left to bottom-right diagonal.
*   **Saccade Reduction:** Minimizing the number of visual focus points to 3 (The Rule of Three) to prevent cognitive overload.
*   **Isolation of Interest:** Utilizing "Depth of Field" (Gaussian Blur) to pull focus towards the primary subject.

### 1.2 Visual Salience Mapping
Utilizing AI-driven salience models to predict the "Probability of Fixation" before publishing a thumbnail.

## 2. Neural Color Grading (CIELAB Space)
Standardizing on color palettes that trigger specific neurochemical responses.

### 2.1 The "Urgency" Palette
*   **Color Math:** High-saturation Warm colors (Red/Orange) in the 600-700nm wavelength range trigger cortisol and dopamine release.
*   **Contrast Ratios:** Mandatory 7:1 contrast for text-on-background (WCAG AAA Standard) to ensure legibility on mobile devices.

### 2.2 Complementary Polarization
Utilizing "Orange & Teal" or "Purple & Yellow" color splits to create visual tension and depth in small-scale (thumbnail) formats.

---

## 3. CTR (Click-Through Rate) Statistics & A/B Testing
Moving from "Vibe-based" design to quantitative, data-driven optimization.

### 3.1 Bayesian A/B Model
*   **Logic:** Rather than simple percentages, utilizing Bayesian statistics to calculate the "Probability of Outperformance" for a new thumbnail design.
*   **Stopping Rules:** Terminating tests once a 95% confidence interval is reached, or when the "Value of Information" is lower than the cost of lost views.

### 3.2 Metadata Correlation
Analyzing the relationship between thumbnail "Vibrancy" (numerical score) and "Average View Duration" (AVD) to identify misleading branding.

## 6. Glossary of Visual Psychology Terms
*   **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** The ratio of users who click on a specific link to the number of total users who view the page.
*   **Salience:** The quality by which an item stands out relative to its neighbors.
*   **Gestalt Principles:** Rules that describe how the human eye perceives visual elements as organized patterns.
*   **Chroma:** The perceived intensity of a color.

---

## 7. Mathematical Foundations: The Golden Ratio in Layout
*   **Formula:** $\phi = (1 + \sqrt{5}) / 2 \approx 1.618$.
*   **Implementation:** In 2025, Moltbot uses this ratio to calculate the "Anchor Point" for primary subjects in the viewport, ensuring harmonic balance.

## 8. Troubleshooting & Performance Verification
*   **Visual Noise:** Too many elements causing the eye to wander. *Fix: Remove 30% of background detail.*
*   **False Contrast:** Using colors that look different but have the same "Values" (Brightness). *Fix: Check the thumbnail in Grayscale.*
